Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 10, 2026

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By authorizing, configuring, or using Vortyx on your servers, you agree to the collection, processing, and system management of your data in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree to these protocols, do not utilize our security systems.

1. Information We Collect

> 1.1 Automatically Collected Data

When you interact with Vortyx on Discord, we may automatically collect specific data points provided by the Discord API:

  • User Information: User ID, Username, Discriminator, and Avatar hash.
  • Guild Information: Guild (Server) ID, Name, Member Count, and Role Hierarchy.
  • Message Data: Message content is processed in RAM for command execution and Automod scanning. We do not persistently store message content unless explicitly logged by a user (e.g., in a ticket transcript or audit log feature explicitly enabled by the server admin).

> 1.2 Data Provided by You

  • Configuration Data: Settings you apply to the bot (e.g., Welcome messages, Auto-role configurations, custom prefixes).
  • Payment Information: Transaction IDs and premium tier status (processed via third-party providers; we do not store credit card details).
  • Support Data: Information provided in support tickets for troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • [+] Service Delivery: To operate the bot's core functions (moderation, music playback, temporary voice, auto-nickname formats).
  • [+] Personalization: To remember your server-specific settings and preferences.
  • [+] Security & Safety: To detect and prevent abuse, raids, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • [+] Communications: To inform you about critical updates, security alerts, or changes to this policy.

3. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.

  • Configuration Data: Retained as long as the Bot is in your server. If the Bot is kicked, data may be deleted after 30 days of inactivity.
  • Temporary Data: Message caches for moderation logic, "Snipe" or "Edit Logger" commands are cleared upon bot restart or after a maximum of 24 hours.
  • Premium Data: Stored for legal and accounting purposes for up to 7 years.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers solely for the purpose of operating the Service:

  • Hosting Providers: Powered securely on bothosting.net to ensure continuous service operations and high availability.
  • Database Providers: Securely isolated cloud configurations to protect active modules.
  • Payment Processors: Standard industry partners (e.g., Stripe, PayPal, RazorPay) to process transactions safely.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Regardless of your location, we extend these key privacy rights to all active users:

Right to Access: You can request a copy of the configuration data we hold about you.
Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate settings or information.
Right to Erasure: You can request complete deletion of all configurations from our storage systems.
Right to Restriction: You can ask us to pause or limit processing your configurations.

To execute any actions under these terms, please contact our Support team directly at sqayangamer@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will immediately take steps to scrub such information from our records.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information, including personal data, may be transferred to — and maintained on — servers located outside of your local state, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws might differ. Your consent to this Privacy Policy, followed by your use of Vortyx, represents your agreement to that transfer.
