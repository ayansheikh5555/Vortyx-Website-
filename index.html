Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This section explains what data Vortyx processes and how it handles it.

1. Data Collection

Vortyx does not store permanent logs of your personal messages. To function properly, it temporarily processes standard Discord data such as User IDs, Server IDs, Channel IDs, and Role IDs.

2. Data Usage

The processed data is strictly used to run bot features, such as processing tickets, logging moderation actions, and enforcing Anti-Nuke configurations within your server.

All temporary configuration data can be deleted from our systems upon request, or automatically when the bot is removed (kicked/banned) from your Discord server.
